Compute the break-even point in dollar sales for August.

Has September's break-even point in dollar sales gone up or down from August's break-even point?

Show Solution

The contribution margin ratio for september is:

294,000 / 660,000 = 0.4454

The september contribution margin ratio is lower than the august contribution margin ratio. Since fixed costs stayed the same, this reduction means that the break-even point for September has gone up.

Assume that the sales of the "Good" bong increase by $35,000.

What would be the effect on operating income?

Show Solution

Contribution margin ratio for the 'Good' bong is (80 - (44 + 8))/80 = 0.35. With an increase in sales of $35,000, the change in net income would be:

35,000(0.35) = $12,250

Assume that the sales of the "Best" bong increase by $35,000.

What would be the effect on operating income?

Show Solution

35,000(0.55) = $19,250
